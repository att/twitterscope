Twitterscope

TwitterScope is a visual monitor of tweets in real time. It groups similar messages into ``countries,'' with keyword summaries, using semantic analysis, and dynamic graph clustering/layout/packing algorithms. Mouse over tweets to read them, or over time series graph for history, and click on a tweet or time series to explore the tweet or historical data.
